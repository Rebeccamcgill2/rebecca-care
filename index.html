About Me

Hello! I’m Rebecca – a self-employed solo adventurer with a heart for care. I work independently, offering personalized one-on-one support for your pets, children, and home. I never do group dog walks unless the dogs live together, ensuring full attention and safety at all times.

With a background in teaching and childcare, as well as being an experienced animal carer, fosterer, and lifelong pet owner, I bring compassion, reliability, and tailored care to every service I provide.

I'm DBS checked and fully insured for pet and home care liability.
